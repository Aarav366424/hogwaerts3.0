The houses of hogwarts

Gryfindor

Godric Grifyndor

Gryffindor is one of the four Houses of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was founded by Godric Gryffindor. Gryffindor instructed the Sorting Hat to choose students possessing characteristics he most valued, such as courage, chivalry, and determination, to be sorted into his house.



Slytherin

Salazar Slytherin

Slytherin is one of the four houses of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was founded by Salazar Slytherin. Slytherins are known for being cunning and ambitious, although it is also known to have produced many Dark witches and wizards.



hufflepuff

helga hufflepuff

Hufflepuff House is one of the four Houses of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was founded by Helga Hufflepuff. Hufflepuffs are known for being trustworthy, loyal and hardworking. According to J.K. Rowling, Hufflepuff roughly corresponds to the element of earth.

Ravenclaw

Rowena Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw is one of the four Houses of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The House was founded by Rowena Ravenclaw. Ravenclaws are known for their wisdom, cleverness, and wit.

The best spots in the castle

The great hall

The Great Hall in the Hogwarts Castle was the main gathering area in the school, which was located off the Entrance Hall. ... The Great Hall was the main place where students received their meals and daily owl post. They also held certain special events, such as the Sorting ceremony, and the Halloween Feast.

forbidden forest

The Forbidden Forest is a patch of untamed forest that grows on the grounds of Hogwarts School. It is home to many magical creatures, including unicorns and centaurs, and is more or less in the charge of Rubeus Hagrid, Keeper of the Keys and Grounds. Hagrid's hut is located on the very edge of the Forest.

The school

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is a British boarding school of magic for students aged eleven to eighteen, and is the primary setting for the first six books in the Harry Potter series and serves as a major setting in the Wizarding World universe.
